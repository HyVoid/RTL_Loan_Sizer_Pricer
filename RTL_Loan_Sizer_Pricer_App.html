Operational Guide
How this tool is structured, and how a deal moves through it.
How to Use
1
InputOpen Inputs. Fill in loan, property, borrower and risk fields — every change recalculates instantly.
2
VerifyCheck Calc Engine for any Fail or Exception Needed flag before quoting.
3
QuoteOpen Quote Summary for the client-safe, single-page term sheet view.
4
AdjustTune leverage caps and the buy-rate grid in Pricing & Assumptions — every dependent figure updates live.
Workbook Architecture
01 · Guide Operational documentation. No calculations live here.
02 · Inputs Single point of data entry — loan, property, borrower, and transaction terms.
03 · Pricing & Assumptions Leverage caps, spread floor, fee defaults, and the FICO × LTV buy-rate matrix.
04 · Calc Engine All leverage math, eligibility gating, pricing lookup, and settlement calculations.
05 · Quote Summary is the only client-facing view — it never surfaces Buy Rate, Lender Spread, or Lender Profit. Every figure on that page is a live formula reference back to Calc Engine, never a typed-in value.
